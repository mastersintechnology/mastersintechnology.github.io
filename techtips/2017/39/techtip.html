Changing the Audio Output Source on Mac

Have you ever wanted to listen to music through headphones even though you had speakers plugged in? You can do so without unplugging your speakers. Open System Preferences and click 'Sound.'





All the possible output sources will appear in the Output tab. Click on one to play audio through that device. Note that picking a new output source can change the volume settings. Be careful to avoid accidentally playing any audio at extreme volume.



You can hold down the Option key and click the volume control in the menu bar to quickly access sound preferences. Find out more about modifier keys here.
