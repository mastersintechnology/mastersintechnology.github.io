How to use iMovie efficiently and effectively

Import your videos

1. Go to file on the top left corner of your toolbar and click "Import media". Search for your videos or a folder containing all the videos.

2. If you wish to record them live, then just use the built-in iMovie camera.


Add the videos to your project

1. Go to file again and select "New movie", again, name the project a meaningful name, for example, "BeachCompilation".

2. Start dragging in videos from your events and move them around so they are in the desired order.

3. If the video is too long, go to the border of the video inside the project and drag it when you see a one directional arrow pop up, then drag to compress.


Add title cards/description boxes to your project

1. Click the 'Titles' tab on the top bar

2. Select a desired title card or description box

3. If it's a title card, drag it beside the videos inside the project and add a text to it

4. If it's a description box, drag it over the video you wish to have a description


Adding audio to your video

1. Click on the 'Audio' tab on the top bar

2. Select your audio from either iTunes, Garage band, or the provided sound effects

3. Drag the audio below the video track


Adding transitions to your video

1. Click on the 'Transitions' tab on the top bar

2. Drag your desired transition between two different video clips.
